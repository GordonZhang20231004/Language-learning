## 日常对话1

    You'll enjoy---先说感受
    working up some sweat ---后说具体的事情
            right after --- 时间关系补充
                sitting at your desk ---场景展开---先动作后地点
                in front of the computer ---上一层补充
                    all day ---时间长度后置
                    five days a week.---最后时间频率

    weekends ---先限定时间范围
    is actually ---强调
        when I do ---后动作
        most of --- 频率
        my working out ---频率补充

     it'd be easier---先整体感受
     to do it ---后内容
        near home ---地点
        than drive downtown.---最后比较 
    
## 日常对话2

    Did you manage --- 事情成功没
        to fix ---具体啥事
        your computer? ---最后对象

    it's still acting up. --- 先说状态
        I think --- 缓冲
            I need professional help. --- 再说明

    I'll email it --- 核心动作先完成
        to you --- 后补充动作对象
            right after ---先给时间关系
            this meeting ---什么样的时间关系


## 公告短文

    We are excited --- 先情绪
    to invite you --- 再动作
        to the annual science fair --- 动作补充
            next Friday ---先大时间
            at 3 P.M. --- 后小时间
            in the university gym.    

    There will be exhibits --- 先建立存在
        from various departments --- 补充限定
            showcasing innovative projects --- 后内容补充
                in the fields --- 限定领域
                of biology, physics, and chemistry. --- 领域信息后置 

    There will be lectures --- 先建立存在
        on scientific topics --- 主体补充
            aimed at a general audience. --- 对象定位
