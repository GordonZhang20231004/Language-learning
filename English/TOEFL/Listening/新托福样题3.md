# Module 1

## 日常对话1
You'll enjoy working up some sweat right after sitting at your desk in front of the computer all day, five days a week.
    You'll enjoy---先说感受
        working up some sweat ---后说具体的事情
        right after --- 时间关系补充
        sitting at your desk ---场景展开---先动作后地点
        in front of the computer ---上一层补充
        all day ---时间长度后置
        five days a week.---最后时间频率

it'd be easier to do it near home than drive downtown.
     it'd be easier---先整体感受
     to do it near home ---后解释---动作后地点
     than drive downtown.---最后比较
