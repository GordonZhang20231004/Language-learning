👉 题目：描述一次你学到重要东西的经历
从我的经历来看，我认为在工作中保持冷静是非常重要的。

这是因为当一个人慌张的时候，很容易出错，也很难处理好事情。

举个例子，就在昨天，我在一家烤肉店打工。因为前一天没怎么睡好，我当时状态不是很好。刚到店里，还没准备好，预约的客人就提前到了。由于我对流程不熟悉，也不会做酒水，当时非常紧张，整个过程都很混乱。不过好在客人没有抱怨。

通过这件事，我意识到，即使不熟练，只要保持冷静，说话慢一点、清晰一点，也能给别人一种可靠的感觉。

所以我认为，保持冷静是一项非常重要的能力。

From my experience, I think staying calm is extremely important, especially at work.

This is mainly because when you panic, you are more likely to make mistakes and lose control of the situation.

For example, yesterday I worked at a  yakiniku restaurant, I didn't get enough sleep the night before, so I was already a bit tired. Right after I arrived, a customer came earlier than expected, even before I finished getting ready. Since I wasn't familiar with the workflow and didn't know how to prepare drinks, I became very nervous and everything got messy. However, the customer didn't complain, which made me realize something important.

After reflecting on this experience, I learned that even if you are not experienced, staying calm, speaking slowly, and being clear complain give others a sense of confidence.

So, i believe staying calm is a very important skill in real-life situations.
