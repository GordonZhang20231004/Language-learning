# 新托福样题3（工作与生活平衡）
## Q1：Interviewer: Thank you for participating. Today, I'd like to ask you some questions about your work-life balance. First, can you share one or two strategies that you use that you think are effective in managing your work-life balance?
    To manage my work-life balance, I use two simple strategies.

    First, I make a schedule for studying, work, and personal activities. This helps me stay organized and use my time more efficiently.

    Second, I try to limit how often I check my phone, especially in the evening. This gives me more time to relax and exercise.

    As an international student, I need to balance classes, TOEFL preparation, and my part-time job. These strategies help me manage my daily life effectively.


## Q2：Interviewer: I see. Many companies are now developing programs to help employees manage work-life balance. Would programs like this affect your interest in working for a particular company? Why or why not?
    Absolutely. Programs like this would definitely make a company more attractive to me because I think work-life balance is very important. It helps people stay productive.  

    For example, as a university student, I need to balance my classes, TOEFL preparation, and part-time job. From this experience, I’ve learned that people perform much better when they have enough time to rest.

    If a company offers flexible schedules or wellness programs, employees are likely to feel less stressed and have more time to rest.

    That's why I would be more interested in working for that company.

Some companies also offer flexible working hours or remote work options to help employees achieve a better work-life balance, but they are concerned that these options would reduce employee attention to tasks or engagement in the workplace.     Overall, I think these programs benefit both employees and companies.
