
# 样题1 A new poetry magazine has asked its readers for submissions, and you decided to submit two of your poems. However, you had a problem using the online submission form, and you are not certain that your submissions were received.Write an email to the editor of the magazine. In your email, do the following.
Tell the editor what you like about the new magazine.
Describe the problem you experienced.
Ask about the status of your submissions.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.

Answer:

    Dear Editor
    I am writing regarding my recent attempt to submit two poems to your new magazine. First, I want to express my admiration for your new publication. I particularly appreciate its focus on contemporary voices and the vibrant layout that makes each poem stand out—it feels like a fresh addition to the literary scene.

    Unfortunately, I encountered an issue with the online submission form. After uploading my files and filling in the required details, I clicked the 'Submit' button, but the page froze without displaying a confirmation message. I tried refreshing and resubmitting, but the same problem occurred, leaving me uncertain if my submissions went through. 
    
    Could you please check whether you received my poems? I would be grateful for any update on their status. Thank you for your time and assistance.

    Sincerely,
    Gordon

# 样题2 Your coworker, Kevin, recently recommended a new restaurant for your team to visit. You took the team there, but everyone was disappointed. The food was not as good as expected, and the service was slow.You need to inform Kevin about the situation and discuss future lunch options.Write an email to Kevin. In your email, do the following.
Explain what was wrong with the restaurant.
Describe the team's reaction to the visit.
Suggest alternative lunch arrangements.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.

Answer：
    Dear Kevin,

    Thank you for recommending the new restaurant for our team lunch. We visited it last week, but unfortunately, the experience was disappointing. The food did not meet our expectations in terms of quality and flavor, and the service was notably slow, which made our lunch break longer than planned.

    The team was quite let down by this visit. Many members expressed that they would not return due to the poor food and inefficient service, which affected our overall morale and schedule.

    Moving forward, I suggest we explore other lunch options. Perhaps we could try a different restaurant with better reviews or consider rotating among reliable places we've enjoyed before. Let me know your thoughts on these ideas.

    Best regards,

    Gordon

Explain why you are traveling to the city.
Ask for her recommendations about restaurants and activities.

Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.

    Answer：

    Dear Maria,

    I hope this email finds you well. I'm writing because I'll be traveling to San Francisco next month for a business conference, and I recall you recently visited the city for work. I'd greatly appreciate your insights to make the most of my free time there.

    Gordon

# 样题4 You have been working on a group project with several classmates for a course. One of your group members, Alex, has not been participating actively and has missed several assignments. You want to address this issue and find a solution. Write an email to Alex. In your email, do the following.
Describe the current progress of the group project.
Explain how his lack of participation is affecting the group.
Suggest ways he can contribute to the project moving forward.
Write as much as you can and in complete sentences.

    Answer：
    Dear Alex,

    I hope this email finds you well. I'm writing to discuss our group project for the course. We've made good progress so far: we've completed the initial research phase and drafted the first two sections of our report. However, we're now approaching the final deadline, and we need everyone's full participation to finish strong.

    Your lack of involvement has been concerning. You've missed several key assignments, such as the data analysis task and the recent group meeting, which has delayed our timeline. This puts extra pressure on the rest of us to cover the gaps, potentially affecting the quality of our final submission.

    To move forward, I suggest you take on specific tasks that align with your strengths. For example, you could handle the conclusion section or assist with editing and proofreading. Please let me know your availability so we can schedule a meeting to discuss this further and redistribute responsibilities. Your contribution is crucial for our success.

    Best regards,

    Gordon

Describe the maintenance issue you have observed in the park.
Explain why it is important to address the issue.

    I am writing as a concerned member of our neighborhood association to bring to your attention a maintenance issue at the local park.     I suggest organizing a community volunteer day to repair the benches and repaint the equipment. This approach would not only fix the problems cost-effectively but also foster a sense of community involvement. Please let me know if you would like to discuss this further.

    Sincerely,

    Gordon
